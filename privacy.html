Privacy Policy — GigantoStudio

Last updated: 12 July 2026

GigantoStudio ("we", "us") operates the GigantoStudio Promo Publisher application (the "App"). The App is a first-party tool used exclusively by GigantoStudio to publish short promotional videos of our own products to our own social media accounts, including our TikTok account.

1. Information we collect

The App accesses only our own TikTok account through TikTok's official APIs, with permissions limited to basic account information (user.info.basic) and video publishing (video.publish). The App does not collect, store, or process any personal information from any other TikTok users, viewers, or third parties. We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics that identify individuals.

2. How we use information

Account access tokens are used solely to upload and publish our own promotional video content to our own TikTok account. Tokens are stored securely on our private, local infrastructure and are never shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party.

3. Data retention

Access tokens are retained only for as long as needed to operate the App and are deleted when no longer required. No viewer or third-party personal data is retained, because none is collected.

4. Third-party services

The App interacts with the TikTok API, operated by TikTok Pte. Ltd. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

5. Children's privacy

The App is a business tool and is not directed at children. It does not collect information from anyone, including children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
